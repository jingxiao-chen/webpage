Who Am I?

Hi I'm Jingxiao Chen I am a Ph.D. student in biostatistics at UTHealth in Houston and research assistant at Department of Bioinformatics and Computational Biology, MD Anderson Cancer Center. My research is mainly on understanding tumor microenvironment (TME) convoluted by tumor heterogeneity and single-cell RNAseq analysis.

I grew up in Hangzhou, Zhejiang Province, China and moved to the United States for college in 2012.
